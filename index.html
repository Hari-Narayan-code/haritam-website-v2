A Taste of Heritage

In a quiet corner of Mylapore, Haritam brings back the Forgotten Flavours of South India. Our kitchen cooks the way homes once did—patiently, from scratch, with seasonal ingredients and clarity of taste. We’re proudly vegetarian, with millet-based preparations and transparent diabetic-friendly choices.

The space feels like a refined Tamil veedu: lime-washed walls, Athangudi tile inlays, brass and wood details, and warm, welcoming light. We now host guests exclusively in Mylapore, so every visit meets the standard you expect.
